Crystals Collector!

Instructions:

  1. At the start of the game, you'll be given a target score.
  2. On the four gemstones pictured below, each one has a hidden value within it. Every time you click on a gemstone, the values are added together. But don't go over the target score or you'll lose the game. Match the score and you win the game.
  3. The target score and the value of the gemstones reset at the beginning of each game.
  4. Have fun!
